Main problems in learning

Neither at school nor at the institute do they teach us how to study the material correctly. We are preparing for exams. We train ourselves to solve repetitive problems in order to do better on a test, but often in the end this does not give us real knowledge. Cramming quickly disappears and does not benefit.

Conclusion: you also need to be able to learn, but for some reason this is not taught anywhere. What to do with it?

Specific techniques and exercises help to change the approach to learning to make it effective and exciting. The same techniques are applied in the example of training in the Practicum.

Learning Techniques

Five practices from Barbara Oakley

Two types of attention

Deep knowledge arises by alternating between focused and diffuse thinking.

Recall

Recall what you have learned - this will allow you to connect disparate portions of memory.

Interliving

Learn several skills at the same time, they enrich each other.

Questions

As you listen to your teacher, come up with a good question that you really care about.

Illusion of competence

Repeat to yourself, write it down, tell a friend: you just feel like you own a new topic.

For those who like to procrastinate

Barbara Oakley's story.

Since the childhood, Barbara was not given mathematics. She considered herself a complete humanist, and far from being the smartest. In the army, she studied Russian to get a raise, so successfully that she was promoted to commander. But for promotion, you had to take math. And then Barbara came up with her own approach to the exact sciences. It turned out that if something is “badly given to you”, your knowledge gained by labor is much deeper than that of those who are clear at first sight.
